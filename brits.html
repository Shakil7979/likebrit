About Brit’s Home

Brit’s Home is a 19,000-square-foot orphanage in Haiti, designed to symbolize Britney’s spirit and commitment. Built to high seismic standards, it offers a safe, nurturing environment for children, providing food, shelter, education, and emotional support. With a family-like atmosphere and dedicated staff, children receive holistic care, including education at Brit's Academy, recreational activities, and medical care. The home embodies Brit’s legacy, aiming to transform lives and uplift the community. Join us in honoring Brit’s dream and supporting these children’s futures.

Brit’s Home is more than an orphanage; it’s a place where children find love, security, and the opportunity to grow into resilient, self-sufficient individuals.

Brit's Academy offers a tailored educational experience, ensuring each child receives the attention and resources they need to succeed academically. With dedicated teachers and a supportive environment, our students are empowered to reach their full potential.

Sustainability

Our health and wellness program provides regular medical checkups, vaccinations, and nutritious meals to ensure the children at Brit’s Home grow up healthy and strong. We prioritize both physical and mental health to support overall wellbeing.

Transition Program

Brit's Clinic

Brit's clinic, a 1,150 sq. ft. facility staffed by two dedicated nurses, offers essential medical care and preventative services, including check-ups and vaccinations, to ensure the health and well-being of our community.

Community Projects

Grand Goâve has been vital to Brit's Home, supporting its growth and employment opportunities. We involve local staff in developing care plans and work with neighboring communities. Since 2013, we’ve distributed 1,000 gallons of clean water daily, with plans to double this amount. While our medical clinics are paused, we look forward to resuming essential health services soon.

Operations Center

Capabilities

The OC allows Be Like Brit to further strengthen the relationship with the local US community, collaborate with volunteers, communicate with Brit's Home in Haiti, store donated items in the 1,100 square foot warehouse, and utilize the four offices, volunteer room and conference room to effectively run the internal operations.

Operations Center

The OC

One of Britney’s favorite shows growing up was The OC, so fittingly Be Like Brit’s Operation Center has become known as “The OC.” We are located at 66 Pullman Street in Worcester, Massachusetts. Originally, Be Like Brit’s headquarters was the kitchen table at the Gengel’s home in Holden, but thanks to dedicated supporters, The OC is now a fully equipped office that enhances Be Like Brit's ability to continue Britney’s compassion.
